Financial Analyst 
Location: Lisbon, Portugal 
Department: Financial Services 
Experience Level: Staff 
 
About the Role: 
Join our Financial Services team as a Financial Analyst. You will support 
financial planning and analysis activities, providing insights to drive 
business performance. 
 
Key Responsibilities: 
 
Assist in the preparation of financial reports and forecasts. 
Analyze financial data to identify trends and variances. 
Collaborate with business units to support decision-making. 
Ensure compliance with financial regulations and standards. 
Support ad-hoc financial analysis and projects. 
Requirements: 
 
Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or related field. 
0-2 years of experience in financial analysis or related roles. 
Strong analytical and quantitative skills. 
Proficiency in Excel and financial modeling. 
Fluency in English and Portuguese required.
